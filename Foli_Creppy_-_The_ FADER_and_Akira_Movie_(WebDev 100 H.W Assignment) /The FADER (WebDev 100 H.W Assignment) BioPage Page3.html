About Foli

(Formerly Known As "T'Challa Lovechild")

T'Challa is the lovechild of legendary action star Chuck Norris and Marvel superhero Black Panther. At birth, T'Challa lead a small infant rebellion in the Maternity Ward of Montifoire Hospital and lead the babies from their beds to a vessel on the Hudson River. After 20 days at sea, T'Challa landed on a small, deserted island in the Carribean. He planted a flag in the ground and named the island, Challaland.

Ten years later, Foli left Challaland for mysterious reasons and moved back to New York were he started his life over again under the name Foli Creppy. Proving to be a genius at every endevour he undertook, Foli soon grew bored and hungered for another challange.

Fast-forward 14 years later, Foli discovered the art of "coding" and was fasinated by the vast oppurtunities available in the field and oppurtunities for both personal and mental growth.

Foli Creppy currently resides in the Bronx. He is a 25 year old music/film buff who enjoys reading, cooking, baking, photography, MMA and adventure. He is also currently working in the fashion industry as a visual merchandiser.

His current goals are to find a career in the tech field as a developer in order to fund his travel plans and allow himself more freedom to create the life he dreams of one day having.
